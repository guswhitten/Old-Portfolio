DipTracer PCB schematic

The infinite line charge extends from positive to negative infinity in the +z and -z directions and has a finite charge.
It has an infinitely small radius, making it a 2D concept.
I chose to model the line charge as a 3D cylinder with 1000C charge in order to produce clean E-field lines.
